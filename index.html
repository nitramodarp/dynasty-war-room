💹 QB Arbitrage — 6pt vs 4pt Passing-TD Edge
Your league pays 6 per passing TD; KTC's model pays 4. The points KTC ignores = 2 × projected passing TDs (from Ciely). Edge % = those hidden points as a share of the QB's 4pt output — i.e. how much of his value lives in the currency the market underprices. High Edge % = pure pocket passers the KTC-4pt market underprices → BUY-FROM others / HOLD yours. Low Edge % = rush-tilted QBs KTC already prices fully → selling yours at KTC market is fair. Dual-threats (Allen/Lamar) land mid — much of their value is rushing, which KTC prices correctly.

📊 Value vs Production — Market Price vs Ciely 2026 Projections
Two axes: market value (app 6pt dynasty price) vs production (Ciely projected points/VORP, your exact 6pt/TEP scoring). Div = production %ile − market %ile within position. Big +Div = out-produces its price → BUY. Big −Div = priced above production → SELL. Age-aware: production leads, but a light year-1 projection on a young player is expected and won't fire a sell (Stowers/Klein/rookies). Value is dynasty (multi-year); production is 2026 only — read RB with age in mind.
